MISSION 02
TOXIC TRANSFER
STAY HEALTHY
READ CAREFULLY BEFORE PROCEEDING
1
One player will randomly start as infected and cannot win this challenge.
2
Every 30 seconds, you must pair up with another player by holding hands or tagging them. You cannot pair with the same person twice.
3
If you pair with an infected player, you become infected. The infection spreads silently.
4
Your status updates when the producer triggers a reveal. You start as Healthy — your screen will update if you become infected.
5

MISSION 03
LOCKED IN
ESCAPE QUICKLY AND QUIETLY
READ CAREFULLY BEFORE PROCEEDING
1
Players will be randomly divided into two teams of 4.
2
Each team races to complete an escape room and unlock a lock.
3
The first team to unlock their lock wins and advances to the final stage.
4
In the final stage, the winning team competes individually — each player must use four digits to crack the final lock.
5
The first person to enter the correct four-digit code wins safety and puts their target up for elimination.
MISSION 04
POKER FACE
BLUFF, TRADE, AND STRATEGIZE
READ CAREFULLY BEFORE PROCEEDING
1
Each player will be dealt five random cards from a deck.
2
Players have 5 minutes to trade cards with other players to build the best possible poker hand.
3
At the end of 5 minutes, the player with the best poker hand wins immunity and puts their target up for elimination.
4
All trades must be done face down — you could trick someone, or be deceived and handed the wrong card. Trust no one.
MISSION 05
INTEL SCRAMBLE
SABOTAGE OR SECURE
READ CAREFULLY BEFORE PROCEEDING
1
Race across the room to collect one letter tile at a time and bring it back.
2
Place the letter in your own cup to secure it, or drop it in another player's cup to sabotage them — or help an ally.
3
After 3 minutes all players stop. You then have 1 minute 30 seconds to form the longest word possible and submit it below.
4
Scoring: each letter in your word = +2 points. Each unused tile = -1 point.
5
Only collect what you need — extra tiles count against you. Top score wins and puts their target up for elimination.
